Deploy Your PPID Dispora Jabar Portfolio Website

Follow these simple steps to deploy your static website to Netlify Drop - a free hosting service that doesn't require any account creation or setup.

Step 1: Open Netlify Drop

Click the button below to open Netlify Drop in a new tab:

Open Netlify Drop

Step 2: Drag and Drop Your Static Folder

Open your File Explorer and navigate to:

D:\ppidporto\Portfolio\static

Select all files and folders in the static directory, then drag and drop them onto the Netlify Drop page.

Step 3: Wait for Deployment

Netlify will upload your files and deploy your website. This usually takes less than a minute.

Step 4: Your Website is Live!

Once the deployment is complete, Netlify will give you a unique URL where your website is hosted. You can share this URL with others to show them your portfolio website.

The URL will look something like: https://random-name-123456.netlify.app

Note:

This is a temporary deployment. If you want to keep your website online permanently or use a custom domain, you'll need to create a Netlify account. You can do this after deploying by clicking the "Claim this site" button on the Netlify page.
